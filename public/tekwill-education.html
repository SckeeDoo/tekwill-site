Tekwill Academy Kids

Tekwill Academy Kids is a educational center for kids formed to provide a place where children under 18 are encouraged to develop their full potential through education and extracurricular activities in area of Creative Industry. Tekwill Academy Kids is supported by companies from IT and non-IT sector, building together dynamic, unique, and constantly evolving programs.

Objectives:

To create the best learning center for children under 18

in area of Creative Industry for children aged between 7 and 18 years old.

To develop

an efficient learning program adapted to the market needs

To open a magazine for kids

The magazine will include 3 important blocks: story telling and testimonials, CSR projects, and relevant product for kids.

To connect

directly companies and our students for qualitative feedback, relevant projects and inputs

To change

the mindset that IT is just about coding

Currently, Tekwill Academy Kids will focus on 5 big programs

Our most important focus is to provide a place where children under 18 are encouraged to develop their full potential through education and extracurricular activities in area of Creative Industry

After School Program

Oriented for kids 6-9 years old. Their daily involvement will be structured in 3 blocks: homework, communication skills in English, Fun Art Lab – non-tech activities.

Robotics course

1 year cycle of study organized on 3 levels: beginners, intermediary, professionals. The main goal is to prepare the participants for international contests.

Programming languages with satellite course

(photography and images editing advanced searching skills) – participants will have to choose from 2 directions of programming: web and applications development. In order to provide a complete experience will learn about useful tools and to generate the most creative products. The most performing participants will be registered to Infomatrix (contest in Romania).

Game development club

the goal is to connect visual art and technologies through will be created a strong community which will represent our country on important international contests: Global Game Jam, Ludum Dare, Coding Game. Additionally, the participants will be involved in a CSR project: design an application for the kids with autism to encourage the communication and social life.

Animation course

will connect technologies with all the types of arts (music, painting, dance, etc.) in order to provide a product. Additionally, will learn about technics of creative writing, online literacy and others.

Fun math and Physics

the goal of this course is to explain math and physics in easy language, puzzles, games, quizzes and special kits, grow the interest of kids for this field and make the connection with engineering and informatics.

Product Design for parents.

A successful educational program is a conjunction between participants, mentors and parents. This is why is crucial to involve the parents as a target group in the learning process through specific workshops, trainings and events. The content will be designed with 3 main topics: efficient comunication, common work with the child for personal goals` achievement, opportunities for future.
In this way the parents will have the competence to:
- easily understand the child`s needs
- early identify basic capacities in technologies and arts
- promote the involvement in this sector and the ideea that IT is not just coding
- help the child to set the purpose driven actions

Magazine for kids

The magazine will be first IT oriented magazine for kids and will have the role to:
- promote the success stories
- stimulate girls` involvement in creative industry
- create and implement social projects for youth under 18
- connect the companies from same or different for a common course/ project (ex. companies in animations design, where the competition is too big)
- will be the most representative promotion channel
- added value for our partners

The magazine will include 3 important blocks: story telling and testimonials (will show the results of the courses), CSR projects (realized with involvement of IT/ non-IT companies), and relevant product for kids (including IT products, opportunities for youth, TV channels for kids, etc.). The magazine will have monthly edition, in number of 1000, being distributed in schools, kindergardens, centers for kids and other public places, in order to achieve the visibility. Also, will have the option of download on the website.
