Tekwill Edcuation have been created in order to help bridge the gap between what is learned in IT education and what is necessary in the industry, Tekwill developed programs for different target groups .The program seeks to develop local ICT talent, transfer knowledge, promote a greater understanding of, and interest in, the technological sector and encourage regional building and participation in the digital community.

Rockstart Launchtrack, Powered by Tekwill
