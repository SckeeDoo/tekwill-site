F5 Internships

Mentors will lead teams to success by guiding and advising students throughout this program.

We’re aiming to create a students-centered space where college students and recent graduates can work in teams to create apps, develop new skills, build professional relationships, and have a great time in the process. One crucial aspect of this event is mentorship—individuals who can offer technological and teambuilding expertise for our interns. One goal of F5 is to provide computer science students with mentorship and networking opportunities, both among each other and with tech developers. Mentors inspire, teach, and lead students to prosperity in a way that creates long-lasting professional relationships, and get to have a lot of fun along the way.

What we offer

Mentorship

Our mentors:

- Have strong skills set in a tech-related domain, such as software engineering, web design/development, game design/development, app building, or hardware

- Approach team facilitation and problem solving with energy and enthusiasm

- Are present for at least 5 hours/week during the entire program (3 months) for troubleshooting and general or domain-specific advice.

Trainings

Helping

During the internship program we are helping participants prepare for the future economy. The series of courses on soft skills will help you build, develop and hone the essential skills needed to improve your employability and advancement in today’s dynamic workforce.

Practice

Focus

You’ll focus your communication, management, negotiation, problem solving, business writing, time management, finance, entrepreneurship, and project management skills into a single project that demonstrates your career readiness.
